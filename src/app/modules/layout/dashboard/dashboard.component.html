Pet

Information about our application
Pet is an application for monitoring and controlling your pet regardless of its species. If your goal is to take care of it, this application will help you:
  • Record of basic information about your pet.
  • Follow-up control from your designated veterinarian.
  • Average and dose that should be applied to your pet.
  • Follow-ups to periodic baths and advice on how to apply them more easily to your pet.
  • Care treatment for your pet.
